## Why not "continuing education / new age tools"

"New age" reads as spiritual/woo and erodes enterprise credibility; "continuing
education" sounds like CEU/compliance, narrower and lower-status than the real
work; the phrase is not how buyers or recruiters search. We kept the good kernel,
"tangible outcomes", inside the value prop.

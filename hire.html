How the work runs

A simple sequence, the one the 5% follow.

1

Prioritize

Find the few use cases with real ROI, and say no to the rest. Most failed programs are busy, not focused.

2

Build

Ship tools that fit how people actually work, not generic demos that stall on contact with a real workflow.

3

Adopt

Build the human capability to evaluate, question, and trust what the tools produce. This is the step the 95% skip.

4

Measure

Track time saved, cost cut, and errors reduced. Adoption rates and license counts are vanity, not value.

Common questions

What is a fractional Chief AI Officer?
Senior AI leadership on a part-time or contract basis. The role sets AI priorities, oversees the build, and owns adoption, without the cost or commitment of a full-time executive hire.
How long is a typical engagement?
An AI Adoption Sprint runs over a few focused weeks around a single pilot. Fractional leadership runs as an ongoing monthly engagement. Keynotes and workshops are scoped to the event. Every engagement is sized to the outcome it targets.
Is the work remote or on-site?
Both. Engagements run remotely, with on-site sessions when a workshop or leadership offsite calls for it.

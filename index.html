The Thesis

Every organization is acquiring AI faster than its people can think with it.

Organizations are only as good as the expert context they have to operate with. The bottleneck is never the technology. It's the capacity of the humans using it. I help extract that context, upgrade how teams think, and bring organizations into the future while keeping humans at the center.

The Complexity Gap

AI capability is growing exponentially. Human cognitive capacity is not growing at all. The distance between what your tools can produce and what your people can evaluate is the defining crisis of this decade.

Cognitive Offloading

When people outsource the process of thinking to AI, they don't just save effort. They prevent growth. Harvard research found that elite consultants relying on AI were 19 percentage points less accurate on tasks at the edge of its capabilities.

The Factory Mind

Our education system was designed in 1806 to produce compliant workers. AI now executes compliance faster than any human. We need to stop producing yesterday's thinkers and start building the thinkers the future actually needs.

Universal Basic Upgrading

AI literacy is horizontal. It spreads across the surface. What organizations actually need is vertical cognitive development: building the capacity to think above the tools, not just operate them.

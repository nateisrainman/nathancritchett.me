About

Nathan Critchett

Cognitive Strategist & Founder / Cognitive Strategist for the AI Era

Nathan Critchett is a cognitive strategist and builder. His work centers on one problem: the widening gap between what AI can produce and what the humans using it can actually evaluate, and how to close it by growing the thinker rather than only adding tools.

He writes and speaks on cognitive strategy for the AI era, and partners with Edapt and Christian Jackson to bring this work to school systems across the country. The throughline of everything here is vertical: build the capacity to think above the machine first, then direct the machine second.

He is a former Division I pitcher and sports-medicine All-American, and he founds tools that protect and develop people, physically and cognitively.
